Keep this list current
This page is a snapshot, not a live feed. I compiled it from journal websites on July 10, 2026 — I don't have a way to auto-refresh it on a schedule. Two easy ways to keep it current:
  1. Ask me to refresh it. Come back to Claude periodically (monthly works well) and ask me to re-search these journals and update the list — I'll rebuild this page with what's newly posted and flag what's closed.
  2. Set up standing alerts so new calls reach the department automatically between refreshes — see the sources below. Most publishers post calls for papers on a dedicated page with no email sign-up, so an RSS reader (e.g., Feedly, Inoreader) or a saved-search Google Alert for "call for papers" public health catches things faster than checking manually.
Journal sources to monitor
Other calls-for-papers finders
  1. JournalTOCs (www.journaltocs.ac.uk) — lets you set up free table-of-contents RSS alerts across most public health journals in one place; searchable by subject.
  2. Wiley, Elsevier, Springer Nature, and Taylor & Francis all maintain "Calls for Papers" landing pages by subject area (Medicine & Public Health) that can be bookmarked and checked on a rotation.
  3. APHA / American Public Health Association newsletters (Inside Public Health) frequently announce AJPH calls before they hit the website.
  4. X/Bluesky and LinkedIn accounts for target journals often post calls before the website is updated — worth a light follow if a few key journals matter most to your department.
